South Oxfordshire

Services

Consultation

Every treatment at BARE by Marlese begins with a personalised consultation. Your tattoo, skin type and medical history are carefully assessed to determine whether laser tattoo removal treatment is suitable for you and to create a safe, tailored treatment plan.

Preparation

Before treatment, the skin is gently cleansed and the treatment area is carefully prepared. Where appropriate, numbing cream may be applied to maximise comfort during your laser tattoo removal session.

Treatment

Advanced laser technology is used to precisely target tattoo pigment within the skin. The laser breaks the ink into microscopic particles allowing your body’s natural processes to gradually clear the pigment between sessions. Most tattoos require multiple sessions spaced 6–8 weeks apart.

Aftercare

Immediately After Treatment

The treated area may feel warm and appear red or slightly swollen. This is normal and usually settles within 24–48 hours.

First Week

Blistering or scabbing may occur as part of the natural healing process. Do not pick or scratch the area.

Avoid

Avoid sun exposure, tanning beds, hot baths, saunas and swimming pools for at least two weeks.

Healing & Sessions

Your body gradually clears the ink particles over time. Treatments are typically spaced 6–8 weeks apart.
