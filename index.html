BARE by Marlese is a VTCT Level 5 certified laser tattoo removal clinic serving clients across South Oxfordshire, including Henley-on-Thames, Wallingford and Didcot. Using advanced laser technology, treatments are tailored to your skin type to safely fade or fully remove unwanted tattoos.

Process

Personal Consultation

Every treatment at BARE by Marlese begins with a personalised consultation. Your tattoo, skin type and medical history are carefully assessed to determine whether treatment is suitable for you and to create a safe, tailored treatment plan.

Careful Preparation

Before treatment, the skin is gently cleansed and the treatment area is carefully prepared. Where appropriate, numbing cream may be applied to maximise comfort during your treatment session.

Advanced Laser Treatment

Advanced laser technology is used to precisely target tattoo pigment within the skin. The laser breaks the ink into microscopic particles, allowing your body’s natural processes to gradually clear the pigment between laser tattoo removal sessions. Most tattoos require multiple sessions spaced 6–8 weeks apart to achieve optimal fading results.

AFTERCARE

Proper aftercare supports optimal healing and the best fading results.

Immediately After Treatment

The treated area may feel warm and appear red or slightly swollen. This is normal and usually settles within 24–48 hours. Keep the area clean and dry and avoid touching unnecessarily.

First Week

Blistering or scabbing may occur as part of the natural healing process. Do not pick or scratch the area as this may increase the risk of scarring.

Avoid

Avoid sun exposure, tanning beds, hot baths, saunas and swimming pools for at least two weeks. Avoid intense exercise for the first 24–48 hours.

Healing & Sessions

Your body gradually clears the ink particles over time. Treatments are typically spaced 6–8 weeks apart to allow the skin to heal and achieve optimal fading results.

FAQ

Does laser tattoo removal hurt?

Most clients describe the sensation as similar to a quick elastic band snap. Where appropriate, numbing cream can be applied to maximise comfort during treatment.

How many sessions will I need?

Most tattoos require multiple sessions spaced 6–8 weeks apart. The number of sessions depends on tattoo size, ink colour, depth and your skin type.

Can all tattoo colours be treated?

Modern laser technology can treat most pigments. During your consultation we assess your tattoo and provide realistic expectations for fading or full removal.

Laser Tattoo Removal Treatment Policies & Booking Information

Tattoo removal is rarely a linear process. Whether your goal is complete removal or fading a tattoo for a cover-up, a personalised treatment plan will be created during your consultation based on your tattoo, skin type, and individual response to treatment.

A minimum interval of 6 weeks between treatments is required to allow the skin to heal properly and for the body to gradually clear fragmented tattoo pigment.

Bundle sessions must be paid in full at the time of purchase and are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.
