Services

Consultation

At Bare, every treatment begins
with a thoughtful consultation.
Your tattoo, skin, and individual
needs are carefully assessed so
your journey feels safe, informed,
and tailored to you.

Preparation

The skin is gently cleansed and
prepared. Where needed, a
numbing cream is applied to
ensure your comfort throughout
the session.

Treatment

Advanced laser technology is
used to target unwanted tattoo
ink with precision. The laser
breaks the ink down into fine
particles, allowing your body to
gradually clear it over time.

Tattoo Removal Aftercare & Healing

Cooling techniques are incorporated to calm the skin and enhance comfort while protecting the
surrounding area.

After treatment, a soothing product is applied and clear aftercare guidance is provided to support
healing and maintain healthy skin between sessions.
